Calculating Tax Rates:
![](https://i.imgur.com/JZXi3Wh.png)
- Statutory Tax Rate: Corporate income tax rate in the country which the company is domiciled
- Effective Tax Rate: Accounting Ratio (Reported Income Tax expense/Pretax Income)
  Useful for forecasting NI
- Cash Tax Rate: Accounting Ratio (Tax paid in cash in period/pretax income)
  Useful for forecasting CF
